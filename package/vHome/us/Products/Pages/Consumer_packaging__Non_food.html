Consumer packaging (Non food)

Packaging designed to meet image and functional requirements both in-store and at home

We take a holistic approach to consumer packaging considering the needs of manufacturers, retailers and consumers.

Whatever your product or route to market, we design all our packaging bespoke to your exact product, marketing and supply chain requirements.  

For premium presentation in-store, we offer high-quality printed packaging, maximising brand image and creating consumer appeal.  Our paper-based solutions are also consumer friendly and 100% recyclable. 

Archive Boxes

Archive boxes are designed for the packing, transportation and storage of office documents, records and files. Made from corrugated cardboard, we offer a range of archive boxes in standard sizes and styles such as a ‘One-Piece’ or ‘Easy to Assemble base’. One piece archive boxes consist of a box and a traditional ‘lift off’ lid that are manufactured together making it more cost effective. This box is easy to erect and has a double layer base held in place by the flaps that also provides additional strength to each end of the box.

Bottle Carriers

Bottle carriers provide consumer convenience during the transportation of bottle from store to home. Retailers use bottle carriers to make purchasing multiple bottles as easy as possible for the consumer in order to increase sales. Bottle carriers are easy to handle and offer consumers the flexibility to shop across different types of products and brands with different sizes bottles.

more

Cluster Packs for Bottles

Cluster packs are solid board sleeves that wrap around multiple bottles, grouping them together for sale and providing a convenient carrying feature. The cluster pack sleeve is strategically positioned around part or the whole of the bottle to create a large surface area to communicate and promote your brand or product. The sleeve carries high quality print to maximise brand impact on shelf and drive increased sales.

more

Composite Cardboard Tubes

Composite cardboard tubes are attractive yet protective containers for gift, promotional and premium products. Eye-catching composite tubes add value to your products, improving shelf presence and achieving maximum consumer appeal. Composite containers consist of a tube body made from 100% recycled paper, sealed with a tin plate or plastic base and lid.

more

Flower Boxes

Made from corrugated cardboard or solid board, we manufacture a range of boxes specifically for the export and retail flower market. For the export market all our flower boxes are designed to provide the required amount of water resistance, strength and insulation to meet the physical requirements of your supply chain whether by road or air. The design of the pack, in combination with the selection of the most appropriate board grade, will ensure your flowers arrive at their destination in optimal condition

more

Folding Carton Packaging

Folding carton (also known as box board) is a low grammage solid board ideally suited to the packaging of lightweight products. We offer a range of folding carton styles such as boxes and trays all using a variety of folding techniques. These can be manufactured flat or pre-glued for hand or machine erect based on your requirements.

more

Gift Wrap Packaging for Bottles

We design and manufacture a range of gift packs for wine, champagne and other bottled products. These stylish bottle packs are designed for use with hand delivered gifts rather than through mail order. Aimed at small, medium and niche drink retailers, our gift wrap packaging allows you to add value to your products, differentiate them from the competition and also delight your customers. With low minimum order quantities, gift wrap packaging is suitable for private and business gifts around holidays and events.

more

Labelled Boxes

Labelled boxes consist of standard corrugated packaging to which a high quality printed label is applied. This product combines the cost effectiveness of unprinted corrugated boxes with the high impact visual graphics of the label. Labelled boxes add value to your packaging whilst minimizing costs.

more

Moving Boxes

Moving boxes are the optimal packaging solution for consumers requiring protection of personal goods during transit. Made from strong double wall corrugated board, moving boxes are available in a range of standard sizes or bespoke requirements.

more

Multi-pack Carriers

Multi-pack is a cost effective system which fits neatly around the top of bottles to group them together for sale and provides a convenient carrying feature. Using a minimum amount of board, they offer significant cost savings and environmental benefits compared to cluster packs and wraparounds.

more

Office Stationery Products

We manufacture a range of corrugated cardboard products specifically for the stationery and office products market. Aimed at retail, wholesale, mail order and contract stationery suppliers, our products are available in a range of standard sizes or bespoke to exact size, print and strength requirements

more

Perfume Box Fitments

Perfume box fitments provide protection for the transportation, storage and display of perfume and other luxury products. The fitment is specifically designed around the perfume to form a protective and decorative feature between the primary packaging and the outer packaging.

more

Powder Boxes

Powder boxes are used for the transportation and sale of free flowing products such as washing detergent, cat litter and dry pet food. We offer a range of powder boxes ranging from a standard corrugated case to an innovative two-piece solution comprising of a solid board sleeve and a corrugated inlet that are glued together to make one complete box.

more

Single Garment Carrier

Single garment carriers are designed to protect a single garment during storage and transit. They provide the ideal solution for garments of high value such as wedding dresses and are most commonly used by clothing hire, removal and mail order clothing companies.

more

Wardrobe Boxes

Wardrobe boxes are designed for the bulk transport and storage of clothing and other textile products. They are predominantly used by the removal and self-storage markets as well as clothing and textile manufacturers. Made from double wall corrugated board, wardrobe boxes have a hinged door for easy access and can be fitted with a plastic or metal hanging rail. The rail allows clothing to be hung to prevent any creasing or damage whilst in transit.
