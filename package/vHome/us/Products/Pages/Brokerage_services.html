Brokerage services

​We provide paperboard brokerage services to help you acquire the products you need to support your business.

Our Independent Paperboard Marketing (IPM) Division is one of largest paperboard brokerages in North America with a reputation for integrity and commitment. 

Our brokerage team has longstanding relationships with the most reliable supplier base in the industry and is ready to assist you in securing products ranging from raw materials to finished goods.

We provide a world class service at competitive prices for the procurement and delivery of the following materials:

  • Containerboard
  • High Performance Containerboard Specialty Grades
  • Specialty Coated Products
  • Gypsum-Rolls & Ribbons
  • Bogus Kraft
  • Coated Papers
  • Solid Fiber Sheets
  • CRB, URB, C1S SBS
  • Poly-Coated SBS

With offices and sales professionals across the continent, we will work closely with you to devise solutions to your most difficult product procurement and supply chain issues. 

Through our extensive brokerage capabilities, we have been the choice of the most demanding corrugators, folding carton manufactures and paperboard converters in our marketplace.

Benefits

  • Unrivalled knowledge of paper and board grades
  • Access to the largest possible base of suppliers to source the best material for your needs
  • Ability to negotiate the best rates through our established suppliers relationships  
  • Reduced admin costs from using a single source supplier 
  • Expert advice on operational and supply chain issues
  • Solutions tailored to assist you in running your business efficiently
 

Services

We offer a range of services to support you in developing the best packaging solution for your products, supply chain and customers.
