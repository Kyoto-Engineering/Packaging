Fruit and vegetable packaging

Sustainable packaging solutions that deliver protection, branding and speed to market

As a leading supplier of packaging to the fresh produce market, we understand the needs of growers, packers and retailers.

Our range of packaging, supported by our packaging machinery expertise, aims to improve the speed of packing lines, maximise space both in-transit and in-store and reduce product damage.

Made from corrugated or solid board, all our packaging can be branded, allowing you to differentiate your products in a competitive retail environment and attract consumer attention.

Bag-in-Box (BIB)

Bag-in-Box (BIB) packaging is designed to extend the shelf life of liquid or semi-liquid food products or as a convenient packaging solution for industrial products. Made from corrugated cardboard or solid board, the outer box provides exceptional protection during transit. High quality printed packs use colour and graphics to attract consumer attention in the retail environment leading to increased sales.

more

Bulk Liquid Containers

Bulk liquid containers offer a heavy-duty packaging solution for the safe storage and transit of bulk liquids. Made from heavy-duty corrugated cardboard, the outer box provides exceptional protection during transit. Part of the Bag-in-Box family, bulk liquid containers incorporate a bag that is specifically constructed to preserve the exact liquid inside, ensuring the contents stay uncontaminated by the air outside.

more

Cases

Standard cases and boxes, also known as American cases, FEFCO 0201s or regular slotted containers (RSC), are the most common styles of packaging used for transporting goods. The standard case provides superb protection and has great stacking strength to endure the demands of the supply chain, eliminating product damage during transit.

more

Counter Displays

Counter displays hold products from which a consumer will shop and are designed to sit on top of retail counters. They are strategically placed at the point of purchase to maximize product visibility and create impulse sales.

more

Folding Carton Packaging

Folding carton (also known as box board) is a low grammage solid board ideally suited to the packaging of lightweight products. We offer a range of folding carton styles such as boxes and trays all using a variety of folding techniques. These can be manufactured flat or pre-glued for hand or machine erect based on your requirements.

more

Free Standing Display Units (FSDUs)

Free-standing display units (FSDUs) are standalone off shelf displays, holding products from which a consumer will shop, and are used in retail environments to influence consumer buying patterns. FSDUs are designed to increase product visibility and maximise brand impact at the point of purchase. Eye-catching displays allow brands to differentiate themselves over the competition and drive increased sales.

more

Fresh Produce Tray (P84)

The P84 system is a family of fruit and vegetable trays. Each tray has a common footprint with interlocking points making it easy to stack. P84s allow you to stack trays of mixed loads and sizes resulting in a cost effective and efficient system for the packaging and transportation of fresh produce. Stacking the P84 trays also increases the stability of loads, reducing damage to fruit and vegetables during transit.

more

Fruit and Vegetable Boxes (Hand Erect)

We manufacture a wide range of hand erect trays and boxes specifically for fruit and vegetables. Hand erect trays are ideal where no machines are available, such as field packing.

more

Fruit and Vegetable Boxes (Machine Erect)

We manufacture a wide range of machine erect trays and boxes specifically for fruit and vegetables. Machine erect trays waste no time with manual assembly, enabling you to process high volumes of trays on site, saving both time and money. Made from either corrugated or solid board, we provide trays with common footprints or made bespoke to your exact requirements.

more

Fruit Punnets

Fruit punnets protect fruit in transit while offering consumers excellent visibility of the product. Paper-based punnets, trays and baskets offer a replacement to polystyrene or plastic trays.

more

Hybrid Retail Ready Packaging

Hybrid retail ready packaging is a dual material two piece solution comprising of a solid board base with a corrugated hood that is glued together to make a complete pack. This packaging combines the supply chain performance of corrugated with the in-store performance of solid board to create the ultimate retail-ready solution

more

Layer Pads

Layer pads are used inside cases to divide layers of stacked products such as cans, bottles and other rigid packaging. The layer pad stabilizes the products and disperses weight evenly to ensure they remain free from damage during transit.

more

Liquid Packaging

Made from corrugated cardboard or solid board, we manufacture a range of packaging specifically for the transportation and storage of bulk liquids. Liquid tight packaging is designed with a special folding technique to ensure it is absolutely leak-proof and uses a recyclable coating system (RCS), which offers the highest barrier performance.

more

Modified Atmosphere Packaging

Modified atmosphere packaging is designed to extend the shelf life of fresh food products. Comprising of a corrugated or solid board tray with a top film, the packaging works by modifying the atmosphere in which the food is packaged.

more

Palletainer

Palletainers are a heavy-duty corrugated solution used for the bulk storage and transit of loose fill products and fresh produce. Palletainers provide a cost effective and environmentally friendly alternative to wooden and metal crates.

more

Pouches

Pouches are an innovative packaging concept designed to extend the shelf life of liquid products such as wine, fruit juices, edible oils and detergents. Branded as Pouch-Up®, our pouches are made from triplex metallised film which has excellent oxygen barrier properties to preserve the liquid inside, ensuring the contents stay uncontaminated by the air outside.

more

Retail Ready Packaging (RRP)

Retail ready packaging (RRP) and shelf ready packaging (SRP) have been developed to get products from case to shelf in one smooth movement rather than decanting single units. SRP primarily considers the shelf replenishment requirements. RRP takes a more holistic approach and considers both shelf replenishment and supply chain requirements, as well as the interaction with the shopper whilst on the shelf.

more

Shaped Corrugated Packaging

Shaped corrugated packaging allows you to incorporate ‘curves’ into your designs. The packaging can be designed to fit around the product without the need to be regular shaped. Innovatively shaped corrugated packaging allows you to differentiate your product from your competitors. The shape, in combination with the high quality print, creates an eye-catching pack, maximising both brand impact and sales.

more

Tray Erectors

Tray erectors automatically form your corrugated cardboard or solid board trays enabling the processing of high volumes of cases saving labor, time and money. We offer a range of tray erecting machinery to suit all your product and tray forming requirements.
