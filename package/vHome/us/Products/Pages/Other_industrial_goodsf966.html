Other industrial packaging

Sustainable packaging solutions to promote and protect your productsÂ 

All our packaging is designed to ensure your products arrive at their destination in optimal condition.

Whether packaging small or large, light or heavy products, we use proven scientific methods to provide fit-for-purpose, cost efficient and sustainable packaging solutions.

Our range of corrugated and solid board packaging products is designed to provide optimal levels of strength and protection to meet the physical requirements of your supply chain.

Air Cushion Packaging

Air cushion packaging is a dual material solution of a corrugated box and plastic film bag that is filled with air. This packaging combines the supply chain performance of corrugated with the lightweight performance of air cushions to create a highly effective transit pack.

more

Cases

Standard cases and boxes, also known as American cases, FEFCO 0201s or regular slotted containers (RSC), are the most common styles of packaging used for transporting goods. The standard case provides superb protection and has great stacking strength to endure the demands of the supply chain, eliminating product damage during transit.

more

Composite Packs

Composite packs consist of multiple materials to create the optimal solution to protect fragile and vulnerable goods whilst in transit. Predominantly made from heavy-duty corrugated board, we use a range of additional materials to support the supply chain requirements such as foam, plastics, polystyrene, plywood and timber.

more

Corrosion Protection Packaging

Corrosion protective packaging contains a special barrier coating designed to protect primary products susceptible to all forms of corrosion. It ensures a product reaches its destination in exactly the same condition as when it was packed, eliminating the rusting or oxidation of products during transport.

more

Corrugated End Caps

Corrugated end caps give unrivalled protection of corners and increase the stability of product (especially flat and fragile products) inside the outer case during transit. Corrugated end caps are an environmentally friendly alternative to using polystyrene end caps.

more

Corrugated Pallets

Corrugated pallets are a lightweight but highly durable alternative to wooden pallets. Made from heavy-duty corrugated cardboard, they are designed to withstand the demands of your entire supply chain. They also provide a solution to international export restrictions which cover the use of wooden pallets, such as ISPM15 (International Standards For Phytosanitary Measures).

more

Corrugated Rolls

Corrugated rolls are a protective packaging product for customers who require a flexible solution to protecting their products in transit. Corrugated board in rolls provides the easiest way to package and protect irregular items of different sizes and forms.

more

Dangerous Goods Packaging

Dangerous goods packaging is designed to provide the highest levels of safety and protection for the transportation of hazardous products. Made from strong corrugated cardboard, dangerous goods packaging provides the required amount of strength to meet the physical requirements of the supply chain whether by road or air. The design of the pack, in combination with the selection of the most appropriate board grade, will ensure your dangerous goods arrive at their destination in optimal condition.

more

Divisions (Corrugated and Solid Board)

Packaging divisions (also frequently referred to as box partitions, dividers or separators) are a type of protective packaging designed to protect multiple individual products (.i.e. bottles) within an outer case, ensuring they remain free from damage during transit. Packaging divisions can be manufactured from both solid board and corrugated board.

more

Easy Glue Packaging

Easy glue packaging is an innovative glue closure system for large format packaging that increases usability and productivity on your packaging line. The glue system allows the relevant joint to be pressed together and stuck in place, creating easier handling on packing lines and eliminating the need for other materials to be used for closure (i.e. stitching).

more

Edge Protectors

Edge protectors are designed to protect, stabilize and reinforce palletized loads during transit and storage. They target the edges that are most vulnerable to damage when in the supply chain.

more

Fanfold

Fanfold is a continuous sheet of corrugated board that has been scored and folded like a fan. It is an ideal solution for customers who have many different sized products and are using a large number of different packaging specifications.

more

Heat Shrink Packaging

Heat shrink packaging is a dual material solution comprising of a corrugated box and a plastic film that is heat shrink wrapped over products. This packaging combines the supply chain performance of corrugated with the stability of shrink wrapping to hold the contents securely in place.

more

Heavy-duty corrugated packaging

Heavy-duty corrugated packaging is designed to give extreme levels of protection in transit and is often used for heavy, fragile, bulky or high value products. Predominantly made from double or triple wall board, heavy-duty packaging can also incorporate a range of additional materials to support the supply chain requirements including foam, plastics, polystyrene, plywood and timber.

more

Hexacomb packaging

Honeycomb packaging is a paper-based material that offers a unique combination of superior strength whilst being light-weight. It provides highest levels of protection due its ability cushion and absorb shock allowing products to transported safely, minimising product damage and returns.

more

Infectious Waste Packaging

Infectious waste packaging allows the safe transportation of potentially hazardous infectious waste products. Produced to meet strict European laws concerning infectious waste risks in transit, packs are suitable for the disposal of all types of potentially dangerous materials that are classified as infectious waste.

more

Integrated pallet packs

Integrated pallet packs are a heavy-duty packaging solution for the transportation and storage of bulk product. This solution performs all the functions of a standard pallet pack, with the added benefit of having a corrugated pallet designed into the pack.

more

Nertop Monoply

Nertop Monoply is produced from 100% unbleached, virgin softwood fibers and is an ideal sustainable packaging solution where high resistance handling and high-speed filling are required. It is recommended for powdered products for high-speed filling without perforation in one-ply valve sacks.

more

Open Mouth Sacks

Open mouth sacks are used for the manual and automated filling of powder, granular and loose fill products. The versatile sacks can be used for industrial products such as cement, lime and adhesives as well as food products such as sugar, flour and animal feeds.

more

Pallet Packs

Pallet packs are a heavy-duty packaging solution for the transportation and storage of bulk products. Made from heavyweight corrugated cardboard, the pallet pack consists of a base, sleeve and a lid that sits on a wooden or corrugated pallet.

more

Palletainer

Palletainers are a heavy-duty corrugated solution used for the bulk storage and transit of loose fill products and fresh produce. Palletainers provide a cost effective and environmentally friendly alternative to wooden and metal crates.

more

Palletizers

Palletizers are designed to automate the placing of full cases onto a pallet, increasing productivity whilst decreasing labor costs and manual handling risks. We offer a range of palletization systems to suit all applications, including robotic, layer and gantry palletizers for both single and multi-line application.

more

Paper Bags - Industrial

Industrial paper bags are used for the manual and automated filling of powder, granular and loose fill products between 1kg - 12.5kgs. The versatile sacks can be used for industrial products such as sugars, seeds and animal feeds. Industrial paper bags are available with a sewn or glued bottom, and for superior strength, reinforcements can be added. Edges can be cut straight or, for higher resistance, a stepped cut is offered.

more

Robotics

Robotics offer maximum flexibility in product handling allowing for products to be collated, packed and palletised in various orientations and patterns, all on the same machine.

more

Safety Box for Syringes

Safety box is an innovative packaging solution created for depositing used vaccination syringes and needles to safeguard against infection and prevent reuse. Branded as the TimSafe safety box, it is designed to meet and achieve the performance, safety and quality requirements of the World Health Organization standards.

more

Scuff Resistant Packaging

Scuff resistant packaging is an ideal way to protect fragile products susceptible to scuffing or scratching during transit. Ideally suited to painted products or those made from glass, plastic or rubber, scuff resistant packaging comprises of a soft non-abrasive liner on corrugated board, which protects products from damage in the supply chain.

more

Single Face Corrugated

Single face corrugated predominantly consists of two layers of paper (an outer liner and fluting) and is supplied in rolls. Single face corrugated is mostly used as inner packaging to separate and protect individual products inside the outer packaging.

more

Slip Sheets

Slip sheets allow you to quickly load and efficiently transport products without the need for pallets. Available in a range of corrugated cardboard or solid board grades, slip sheets are designed to meet the physical requirements of your production environment and the supply chain.

more

Standard Mailing Boxes

We manufacture a range of standard mailing boxes, providing an efficient protective packaging solution for products during distribution. Available in a range of standard sizes, the mailing boxes are available as stock items or manufactured to order.

more

Tamper Proof Packaging

Tamper proof packaging is designed to provide maximum security during the storage and transit of high value or vulnerable goods. Tamper proof packaging includes a number of features, which ensure any interference can be recognized, such as a flat bottom to avoid entry; a printed tear strip on the upper flap that is impossible to replace; and two locking tabs that if broken, drop into the case.

more

Thermal Packaging

Thermal packaging is designed for temperature sensitive products that require a defined temperature to be maintained during transportation to the end user. Thermal packaging comprises of an outer pack made of corrugated board, moulded polyurethane foam and frozen gel packs.

more

Timboard building

Timboardâ„¢ building is a roof base board intended for use as wind protection and water-drainage boards within covered walls and roofing. It is a lightweight yet highly durable alternative to traditional wooden roof deck solutions, offering the same functionality at a lower cost â€“ with potential savings of up to 60%.

more

Totem Displays

Totem displays are lightweight free standing displays used to communicate your marketing messages and attract consumer attention. Often used in retail environments in high-traffic areas, eye-catching totem displays allow brands to differentiate themselves over the competition and drive increased sales.

more

Valve Sacks

Valve sacks are used for the automated filling of powder, granular and loose fill products. The versatile sacks can be used for industrials products such as cement, lime and adhesives as well as food products such as sugar, flour and animal feeds.

more
