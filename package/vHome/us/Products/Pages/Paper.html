Paper

Responsibly supplying raw materials for the sustainable manufacture of paper products

We aim to better understand and anticipate your paper needs, maintain the highest service level and deliver superior value.

For the manufacture of recycled paper, we supply recovered papers. We support the Confederation of European Paper Industries (CEPI) list of standard grades of recovered paper and board EN643.

For the manufacture of kraft paper, we supply wood pulp. The pulp is FSC® certified from eucalyptus trees harvested from well-managed forestry plantations.
