Timboard Furniture

Timboard™ Furniture is a first-class solid board material ideally suited to the ready-to-assemble (RTA) furniture industry. 

Typical furniture applications include:

Back Panels

  • Timboard™ folding back panels for bookcases can reduce the overall weight of the bookcase and also the amount of packaging required

Picture Frame Backs

  • Traditional picture frame panels often have a thickness of >2.5 mm. The standard Timboard™ panels are produced in a 1.7 mm caliper, providing the same strength with less material.

Drawer Bottoms

  • Timboard™ panels can replace hardboard panels as a drawer bottom with the option of high quality print on the drawer bottom to give it a modern look.

Timboard™ is extremely strong, easily foldable and has a smooth surface, which is even suitable for high quality printing. The board can also be laminated with decor papers in the precise color of other furniture parts.

Technical characteristics

  • Minimum Timboard™ size: 100 x 250mm
  • Maximum Timboard™ size: 1600 x 3600mm
  • Thickness range: 0.7 to 4.2mm
  • High quality folding line
  • Free from formaldehyde
  • Strong and smooth surface
  • Suitable for high quality printing
  • 100% recyclable
