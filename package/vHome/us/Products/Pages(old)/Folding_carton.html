Folding carton

Responsibly supplying paper and board for the sustainable manufacture of packaging

We aim to better understand and anticipate your board needs, maintain the highest service level and deliver superior value.

For the manufacture of folding carton or boxboard packaging, we supply folding carton sheet board that is available in a wide range of weights for many different packaging applications and markets

We also have conversion operations able to meet your folding carton packaging requirements.

Clay Coated News Back

Master Clay Coated News Back is a seven-ply product. The top coated ply is made of TiO2, Caolin and Calcium Carbonate.

Clay Coated White Back

Master clay coated white back is a seven-ply product. Top coated ply is made of TiO2, Caolin and Calcium Carbonate. The two back plies are 60% bleached hardwood and 40% recycled fibre.

more

Coated Kraft Back

Coated kraft black is made of several plies and is classified as a multipurpose product. Top Plies: double coating top on two plies of Total Chlorine Free (TCF) bleached eucalyptus fibres. Bottom Plies: several plies are made from a mixture of FSC® certified unbleached virgin softwood and hardwood fibres.

more

Coated White Back

Coated white back is made of several plies and is classified as a multipurpose product.

more

Euroboard Spezial Kraft (ESK)

Euroboard Spezial Kraft (ESK) is a fully coated White Lined Chipboard (WLC) with the reverse side made of pre-consumer waste unbleached sulphate kraft pulp.

more

Euroboard Spezial Weiss (ESW)

Euroboard Spezial Weiss (ESW) is a fully coated White Lined Chipboard (WLC) GT 1 that is FSC® and PEFC certified.

more

Grease Guard food fully sized

Grease Guard Food (ESW) folding carton board has been specifically designed for packaging that is in direct contact with food and is ideally suited for fruit and vegetable packaging. This board can be used as an alternative to virgin fibre board with PE lamination.

more

Grease Guard Food Standard

Grease Guard Food (ESW) folding carton board has been specifically designed for packaging that is in direct contact with food. It is ideally suited to frozen food packaging, bakery packaging and take away packaging, where it can be used as an alternative to virgin fibre board with PE lamination.

more

Grease Guard High

Grease Guard High is fully coated White Lined Chipboard (WLC). The board has a specifically designed grease barrier allowing direct contact with dry, wet and fatty foods.

more

Hansaboard

Hansaboard is a fully coated White Lined Chipboard (WLC) that is FSC® and PEFC certified. Top side: coated, wood-free, white Inner layer: grey Reverse layer: grey

more

Lightly Coated Kraft Back

This product is made of several plies and is classified as a multipurpose product. Top Ply: double coated top on brown FSC certified virgin fibers Bottom Plies: several plies are made from a mixture of FSC certified virgin softwood and recycled fibers. The product is used in folding cartons mainly in consumer products for the food and beverage industry that require basic printing.

more

Multiprint

Multiprint is a fully coated White Lined Chipboard (WLC) that is FSC® certified and available with hard-sized treatment.

more

Top Print

Top Print is a fully coated White Lined Chipboard (WLC) with a grey reverse side. It is FSC® and PEFC certified and available with hard-sized treatment.

more

Top Print K

Top Print is a fully coated White Lined Chipboard (WLC) with a brown reverse side. It is FSC® and PEFC certified and available with hard-sized treatment.

more

TwinCoat

TwinCoat is a fully coated White Lined Chipboard (WLC) GT1 with excellent print capabilities on both the top and reverse side. The product is FSC® and PEFC certified.

more

Uncoated Folding Board

This is a multi-ply product. The top and bottom plies are clean brown color produced with FSC® certified virgin fibers. The product is used in folding cartons mainly in consumer goods such as food and beverage products that require basic printing.
